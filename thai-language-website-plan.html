02 — Architecture

Pages & UX Structure

Every page serves one conversion goal. Map each page to an action before designing anything.

Homepage
Goal → Email signup or Start CTA
Hero with bold headline + social proof numbers. Short "Why Thai is achievable" hook. 3 benefits above the fold. Free lesson teaser or interactive tone demo. Testimonials. FAQ. Final CTA block. Navigation: minimal — logo, courses, blog, login, "Start Free" button.
Courses Page
Goal → Purchase / Trial signup
Tiered layout: Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced. Each course card: level badge, lesson count, duration estimate, preview video thumbnail, price, CTA button. Filter by goal (travel, script reading, conversation). Progress previews for logged-in users.
Lesson / Demo Page
Goal → Upsell to full course
Unlock 1–3 free lessons. Thai script display + romanization toggle. Audio playback with waveform. Vocabulary cards. Mini quiz. Progress bar. Paywall teaser after lesson 3 with "You're doing great — continue?" CTA.
Blog / Resources
Goal → SEO traffic → Email list
Pillar articles: Thai tones guide, Thai script for beginners, common phrases, Thai culture explainers. Each article ends with an opt-in for a free PDF cheat sheet. Category filtering. Related posts. Author bio with credibility markers.
Pricing Page
Goal → Subscription conversion
3-tier pricing (Free, Pro, Lifetime). Annual/monthly toggle with savings callout. Feature comparison table. "Most Popular" badge on middle tier. Money-back guarantee. FAQ on billing. Testimonials near price cards.
About / Story Page
Goal → Trust building
Founder story with Thailand connection. Teacher bios with credentials and photo. Mission statement. Press mentions / media logos. Community size stat. Video tour of the platform.

03 — Conversion Design

High-Converting Elements

These are the specific design and copy decisions that turn visitors into paying students.

TRUST
Social Proof Stack
Student count ("12,000+ learners"), star ratings, photo testimonials with nationality flags, before/after fluency stories, video reviews from real students in Thailand.
URGENCY
Scarcity & Offers
Limited-time enrollment cohorts, countdown timers for launch pricing, "Only 3 spots left in live session" for group classes, seasonal discounts (Songkran, Thai New Year).
LEAD GEN
Lead Magnets
Free PDF: "100 Essential Thai Phrases". Free mini-course: Learn to read Thai in 7 days. Free tone training audio pack. Quiz: "What's your Thai learning style?" → personalized result + email capture.
UX
Friction Removal
No credit card for free trial. Google/Facebook SSO. Progress saved without account. Mobile-first responsive design. Fast page loads under 2s. Sticky CTA bar on mobile.
COPY
Headline Formula
Lead with transformation, not features. "Speak Thai with confidence in 90 days" beats "Learn Thai Online". Address the fear: "Even if you find tones impossible." Specific outcome + timeframe = click.
RETENTION
Gamification Hooks
Daily streaks, XP points, level badges, leaderboards, completion certificates. Progress dashboard after login. Weekly email with learning summary. All reinforce habit formation and reduce churn.

04 — Content Strategy

Curriculum & Content Architecture

Content is the product. Structure it by learning outcome, not arbitrary topics.

Level 0 — Foundations
Free tier / lead magnet
Thai tones explained visually. Basic greetings and polite particles (ครับ/ค่ะ). Numbers 1–100. Days, months, telling time. Introduction to Thai script — vowels and consonants overview. 5 survival phrases with audio.
Level 1 — Beginner
Core paid tier
Full Thai alphabet (44 consonants, 28 vowel forms). Tone rules and tone marks. 500-word core vocabulary. Present tense sentence structure. Shopping, food, transport scenarios. 20+ video lessons with quizzes.
Level 2 — Intermediate
Pro / subscription
Reading real Thai text (signs, menus, social media). Classifiers and particles deep-dive. Past and future tense patterns. 1,500-word vocabulary with spaced repetition. Listening exercises with native speed audio.
Level 3 — Advanced
Premium / live tutoring
Formal vs. colloquial registers. Thai news and media comprehension. Business Thai. Writing practice with AI feedback. 1-on-1 sessions with native tutors. CEFR B2 preparation content.

06 — Growth

SEO & Traffic Strategy

Organic search is the highest-ROI channel for language learning. These are the topics people actually search for.

SEO PILLARS
High-Volume Keywords
"Learn Thai online", "Thai alphabet guide", "Thai tones explained", "how to read Thai", "Thai phrases for travel", "Thai for beginners". Create a dedicated pillar page per topic.
CONTENT HUB
Blog Architecture
Topic clusters: Alphabet → Tones → Vocabulary → Grammar → Culture. Internal linking between clusters. Aim for 2 new articles/week initially. Each ranks independently and feeds the funnel.
SOCIAL
Platform Strategy
TikTok & Instagram Reels: 30-second Thai word-of-the-day clips. YouTube: long-form lessons rank on Google. Pinterest: Thai phrase infographics. These compound over time at zero ad spend.
PAID ADS
Paid Acquisition
Google Ads: target "learn Thai online" with free trial offer. Facebook/Instagram: target expats in Thailand, language learning enthusiasts. Retarget blog readers with course offers. Start with $500/month test budget.
COMMUNITY
Network Effects
Facebook Group, Discord community, or forum on-site. Students helping students extends your content surface area. Alumni stories feed testimonials. Referral programme: "Give 20%, get 20%".
PARTNERS
Affiliate & Collab
Partner with Thailand travel bloggers, expat YouTubers, Muay Thai schools, Thai food channels. Affiliate programme (30% recurring). Guest posts on expat forums. Appear on language learning podcasts.

07 — Execution

Build Roadmap

Four phases from zero to a live, revenue-generating platform. Each phase has clear exit criteria before moving forward.

Phase 1 — Weeks 1–4
Foundation & Validation
Define niche and ICP. Register domain and brand. Build landing page with email capture and free lead magnet. Create 5 sample lessons. Validate demand: 200+ email subscribers before building full product. Set up analytics from day one.
Phase 2 — Weeks 5–10
Core Product Build
Build full website (homepage, courses, blog, pricing, auth). Record Level 1 course (20+ lessons). Integrate Stripe for payments. Launch free tier + one paid course. Publish 8 SEO blog articles. Set up email automation sequences.
Phase 3 — Weeks 11–16
Conversion Optimisation
A/B test homepage headline. Add testimonials and social proof. Implement gamification (streaks, XP). Launch referral programme. Start paid social ads. Interview first 20 paying students for case studies. Add quiz lead magnet.
Phase 4 — Month 5+
Scale & Expand
Add Level 2 & 3 curriculum. Build subscription tier. Introduce live group sessions with tutors. Launch mobile app (React Native or PWA). Explore B2B: Thai language training for companies with Thai operations. Build affiliate programme.
